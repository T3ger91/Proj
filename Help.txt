encountering a monster
    A player can either choose to fight if - press f or escape - press e. 
    if the player wants to fight:
        A fight is done my rolling a die. There ale also weapons that give the player higher chance of beating the monster - the die has a lower number of edges.
        The player gets a random weapon and will see how many sided die they have.
    if the player chooses to escape:
        They sucesfully ran away from the monster
